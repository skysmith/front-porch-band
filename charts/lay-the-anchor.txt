Lay the anchor my boys
 on this chord full of noise
We must sing out our love and our sorrow

There’s a storm, skies are red, 
foggy land up ahead
We lay safely awake in the harbor 

Like the prow cuts the waves 
we shall someday be saved 
And we’ll no longer fear and no suffering 

Yet the girl on the shore
Never knows what’s in store
Making castles of sand waiting for you

She can never forget 
All those nights poorly spent 
And those dozens of soldiers in battle 

On a breath or a prayer
We must someday set sail
Cross the ocean of blue just to meet her

It is hard not to stay
But I must go away
May you never forget I adore thee
